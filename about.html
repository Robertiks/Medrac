What is Medrac?

Medrac is a simple yet powerful Chrome extension designed to help you download media content from websites with just a few clicks. Whether you're looking to save videos, images, audio files, or other media types for offline viewing, Medrac provides a streamlined solution that works across a wide range of popular websites.

Whether you're a content creator collecting resources, a student saving educational materials, or just someone who wants to enjoy media offline, Medrac offers a straightforward way to download and organize web media without complicated steps.

Features

  • One-Click Media Detection: Automatically identifies downloadable media on any webpage and provides easy download options
  • Format Selection: Choose your preferred quality and format when multiple options are available
  • Batch Downloads: Select and download multiple media files simultaneously to save time
  • Download Management: Built-in queue system with progress tracking and download history
  • Comprehensive Format Support: Works with most common media formats including MP4, MP3, WEBM, JPG, PNG, GIF and more
  • Cross-Browser Compatibility: Consistent performance across different versions of Chrome

Real-World Applications

Content creators use Medrac to collect inspiration and resources for their projects. Students download educational videos and lectures for offline study sessions. Travelers save media content before trips to areas with limited connectivity.

Users report significant time savings compared to manual downloading methods, with the extension's intuitive interface making media downloading accessible even to those with limited technical knowledge.

Privacy & Security

Medrac respects your privacy and maintains high security standards:

  • No tracking of your browsing history or downloaded content
  • Minimal permissions required to function effectively
  • No data shared with third parties
  • Regular updates to maintain compatibility and security
  • Open source code for transparency

We operate with a user-first approach and only process the information necessary for the extension to function properly. Your downloaded content remains private and under your control at all times.
